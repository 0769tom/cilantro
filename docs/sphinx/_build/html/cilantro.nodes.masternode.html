cilantro.nodes.masternode.masternode module¶

Masternode These are the entry points to the blockchain and pass messages on throughout the system. They are also the cold storage points for the blockchain once consumption is done by the network.

They have no say as to what is ‘right,’ as governance is ultimately up to the network. However, they can monitor the behavior of nodes and tell the network who is misbehaving.

validate_block_contender(block)[source]¶

Helper method to validate a block contender. For a block contender to be valid it must: 1) Have a provable merkle tree, ie. all nodes must be hash of (left child + right child) 2) Be signed by at least 2/3 of the top 32 delegates :param block_contender: The BlockContender to validate :rtype: bool :return: True if the BlockContender is valid, false otherwise
